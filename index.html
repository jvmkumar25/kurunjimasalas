About Us

We are more than just a purveyor of spices and masalas; we celebrate the purity and genuine intent that lies at the heart of every kitchen. The name Kurunji is rooted in ancient tradition where purity signifies truth and our mission is simple yet profound: to bring the finest 'thooyamayana' spices and masalas to every Tamil household. With us, every dish becomes a heartfelt experience, bringing families together and creating cherished memories through the joy of cooking.
