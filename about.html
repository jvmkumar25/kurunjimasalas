About Kurunji Masala

Kurunji Masala began its journey with a humble investment of Rs. 10,000/-, driven by the vision of its founder,Mrs. Revathi. Starting as a small-scale initiative under CreedVirkchamEnterprise,the brand was built on a passion for authentic flavors and uncompromising quality. In the early days, the focus was on sourcing pure turmeric and other spices directly from trusted farmers, ensuring freshness and purity at every step. Over time,Kurunjiexpanded its offerings from turmeric powder, chilli powder, and coriander powder to a wide range of traditional masala blends crafted to capture the true essence of Indian cooking. Today, Kurunji stands as a symbol of taste, tradition, and trust, bringing the rich flavors of the kitchen to homes across the country.

Our product range includes turmeric powder, chilli powder, idly podi, sambar masala, and many more carefully crafted blends. These are made using traditional grinding methods to ensure maximum aroma, flavor, and shelf life.

Kurunji Masala is more than just a spice – it is a promise of quality, heritage, and authenticity that enhances every meal in your home.

About Kurunji Masala

Founded underCreed Virkcham Enterprises by Mrs. Revathi, Kurunji Masala was born with a vision to deliver authentic, high-quality spices while uplifting communities. Based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, the company began with a small range of turmeric, chilli, and coriander powders, slowly expanding into over 50 varieties of pure spice powders, blended masalas, instant mixes, and other value-added food products. From its inception, quality has been the guiding principle of Kurunji Masala. Every product is made from carefully sourced agricultural produce, adding value for farmers and helping them get fair prices for their harvest. By combiningtraditional recipes with modern processing technology, Kurunji Masala preserves both taste and purity, earning the trust of customers across India and abroad.

We produce a wide range of spice powders, blended masalas, and instant mixes. From handpicked ingredients to traditional grinding methods, every product is crafted with care to preserve aroma, flavor, and purity.

With Kurunji Masala, you don't just cook — you carry forward a legacy of taste, wellness, and authenticity in every dish.

Our Mission

At Kurunji Masala, our mission is to uphold the legacy of authentic Indian cooking by delivering premium quality spice powders and blends. We aim to empower kitchens with traditional flavors, crafted using hygienic processes, pure ingredients, and a deep love for heritage cooking.

We are dedicated to ethical sourcing, uplifting rural farmers, and ensuring every household experiences our passion for purity and taste.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make Kurunji Masala a symbol of trust, purity, and traditional excellence in every part of the world. We aspire to bring the warmth of Indian spices to global kitchens — preserving authenticity while embracing modern quality standards.

We dream of a future where our brand stands for more than flavor — it represents a legacy of love, health, and homegrown excellence.
